Though it is not neccesarily what makes a webpage "pretty," it is an essential component. Then we have Back-end which is more focused on the information within the server. It's what keeps things moving in the right direction data-wise and makes sure the interaction with the webpage is correct. lastly, we have what is called Full Stack, which can be considered a mix of both front-end and back-end.
